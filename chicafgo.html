ER:LC Roleplay Rules

These rules must be followed at all times to ensure fair and realistic roleplay.

  • RDM (Random Deathmatch): Killing others without a valid roleplay reason.
  • VDM (Vehicle Deathmatch): Running players over without roleplay justification.
  • NLR (New Life Rule): You must forget all prior events after you die or respawn.
  • LTAP (Leaving to Avoid Punishment): Leaving the server to avoid tickets or jail time is prohibited.
  • LTAA (Leaving to Avoid Arrest): Logging off while being pursued or arrested is not allowed.
  • Cuff Rushing: Instantly cuffing or tasing without roleplay, commands, or warning.
  • FailRP: Unrealistic actions that break immersion or realism.
  • Cop Baiting: Provoking law enforcement to force chases or scenes.
  • Powergaming: Forcing actions on others without allowing proper reaction.
  • Metagaming: Using out-of-character information your character would not know.
  • Combat Logging: Leaving during an active roleplay or combat situation.
  • No Intent to RP: Trolling or refusing to participate in roleplay scenarios.

Chicago Roleplay Discord Rules

These rules must be followed or will result in punishment by our moderation team.

  • No NSFW: NSFW content is strictly prohibited and will result in a ban.
  • Profanity: No profanity, including shortened or bypassed versions.
  • Respect: All members must be respectful, especially toward boosters.
  • No Spamming: Spamming messages, emojis, or commands is not allowed.
  • No Advertising: Advertising other servers or services without permission is prohibited.
  • Appropriate Names: Usernames and nicknames must not be offensive or inappropriate.
  • Follow Staff Instructions: Staff decisions are final. Arguing may lead to further punishment.
  • No Impersonation: Impersonating staff, users, or public figures is not allowed.
  • Boosters: Boosters receive perks but must follow all rules. Abuse may result in punishment.
